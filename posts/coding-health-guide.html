Eye Strain in the AI Era

health, productivity, ergonomics, wellness

AI development environments create new health challenges. Here are essential strategies for maintaining wellness while coding.

Common Problems

  • Eye strain from constant screen focus
  • Dry eyes from reduced blinking
  • Poor posture from multiple monitors
  • Sleep disruption from blue light exposure

Essential Solutions

Workspace Setup

  • Monitor distance – 20-26 inches from eyes
  • Screen height – Top at or below eye level
  • Lighting – Reduce glare with ambient lighting

Daily Habits

  • 20-20-20 Rule – Every 20 min, look 20 feet away for 20 sec
  • Hourly breaks – 5 minutes away from screen
  • Blink consciously – Keep eyes moist
  • Dark mode – Reduce eye strain

Work Balance

  • Whiteboard planning – Start designs offline
  • Paper notes – Quick thoughts and sketches
  • Walking meetings – Take calls while moving
